Main Features

For the Timekeeper

Schedule automatically synchronized from jw.org, time control with flexibility for adjustments, discreet messages to speakers and dedicated monitor for the podium.

For Speakers

Always visible timer with elapsed and remaining time, clear visual signals and contextual messages from the control room directly on the monitor.

For the Meeting Chairman

Web monitor accessible from smartphone or tablet on the local network, real-time progress monitoring and exportable schedule.
